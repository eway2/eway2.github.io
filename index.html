The Catholic Saints

Some of Emma's Favorite Saints

Saint Joan of Arc: The Follower of God and Leader of Men 9/3/2019

Saint Joan of Arc is one of the most well known Saints in the Catholic Church. She led France into battle against the British and won. After her victory, she was sold to the British and burned at the stake for witch craft.

Saint Agnes of Rome: The Sweet Lamb of the Catholic Church 9/3/2019

Agnes of Rome is a Catholic saint who died when she was 13 as a martyer after she refused to wed. She is the patron saint of young girls, chastity, and rape survivors.

  • Author: Emma Way
  • Comments: 3

Saint Lawrence: The Man who Laughed at Death by Grill 9/3/2019

Saint Lawrence is a saint from the early church. He was a Roman Deacon who was martyred by being grilled alive. It is said that after burning for some time he asked his prosecutors to turn him over because that side was done.

  • Author: Emma Way
  • Comments: 3

Saint Benedict: The Ultimate Hermit and First Monk 9/5/2019

Saint Benedict was a Father of the early Catholic Church and the first to start a monastic order. He started life in wealth, learning rhetoric in a university. When he saw his colleagues using rhetoric for evil, he left school and his fortune and became a hermit. After a few hermit wars, he started the first monastic order and wrote a book that is still used today to format the many orders around the world.

  • Author: Emma Way
